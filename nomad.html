The Journey

Being a digital nomad is not just about traveling — it is about designing a life where curiosity, craft, and freedom reinforce each other. As a full-stack developer and ML engineer, my laptop is my office and the internet is my team channel.

I started sharing this lifestyle on Instagram @imi_______i to document the real side of remote work: early morning commits before a hike, debugging from a rooftop terrace, finding the best Wi-Fi in a new neighborhood, and learning how different places shape how you think and build.
