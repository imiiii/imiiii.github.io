What This Page Is About

This is the home for my digital nomad story on the web — a companion to my Instagram, where I document travel, nature, off-road adventures, healing, and the mindset of someone who codes for a living but refuses to live inside four walls.

My feed mixes English and Persian, cinematic drone shots with raw captions, and hashtags like #digitalnomad, #healingjourney, #nomadlanding, and #drone. It is part travel diary, part creative journal, part reminder to trust the journey.
