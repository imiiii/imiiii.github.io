About Me

I am a full-stack developer and ML engineer with a master's in computer systems from Shahid Beheshti University and research experience at the Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences. I have spent years shipping production software — from enterprise systems and brokerage platforms to Chrome extensions, APIs, and ML pipelines — while exploring how software and hardware meet in deep learning systems.

My background blends systems engineering with practical product development. I care about intermediate representations between high-level and low-level languages, compilers, LLVM, and MLIR — but I am just as happy building a polished web app that solves a real problem for a real user.

Research Project

SPRINT

I designed and developed a framework for using in memory processing technology for DNN hardware acceleration. The framework integrates the procedure required for converting DNN models to executable binaries. This technology enables us to process memory-intensive layers of a DNN, such as fully connected, on memory rather than GPU or CPU.
